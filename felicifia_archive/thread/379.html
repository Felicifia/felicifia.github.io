Hey, just a quick note to say that if any of you feel like a trip to Oxford this month, Peter Singer's coming here to do a few talks. The main one's sold out, the one on Wednesday could well be full before you get there so it's probably not worth the trip if you're not in Oxford (but it's at 5pm in Corpus Christi's MBI AI Jaber Building) and there's one on Friday 20th, 3-4.30pm in the South School in the university's Exam Schools on the High St, where he'll be talking about Giving What We Can and global poverty in general.
