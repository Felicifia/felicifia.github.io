by peterhurford on 2012-11-17T19:19:00

Talk of other ethics forums is completely fine, in my opinion.

I didn't know of any forums off hand, so I did a quick Google search of a few different terms and reviewed some of what came up. The St. James Ethics Centre Forum seems somewhat active, though I cannot tell at all what kind of ethical "framework" they're approaching things from.

There's the Ethics Section of the Philosophy Forum, with a mix of frameworks being applied by different people to questions, some of which are utilitarians.

A very active Christian Ethics forum. Have at it, but be wary of their very limiting rules.

I couldn't find any other active ethics forums, but I didn't search for that long.
