Hey All!

The Centre for Effective Altruism is looking for staff and interns in our latest recruitment round.

We are currently hiring for several full-time paid roles:
- Director of Development for CEA
- Lead Developer for 80,000 Hours
- Careers Analyst for 80,000 Hours
- Director of Community for Giving What We Can

Apply for these roles by 28th February 2014 at 5pm GMT, by filling out this form.

You can read about the specifics of each role on this page. See also our page on why CEA is an excellent place to work.

We are also always looking for interns to join us on our Graduate Volunteer Scheme. We will consider these applications at the same time as we consider those for jobs, so we encourage you to apply by 28th February. Apply by filling out this form. If you are interested in either a job or an internship, you can apply for both.

All jobs and internships are based at our office in the centre of Oxford. We can often provide free accommodation to interns, and rent out accommodation to new employees.
