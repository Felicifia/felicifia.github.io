Distributing vegan pamphlets at an elementary school?

by Elijah on 2013-02-13T04:27:00

My sister is ten and attends an elementary school. She's too akratic to become a vegetarian (unsurprising, since she's ten), but she is sympathetic to the cause. I wonder if it would be okay to give her vegan pamphlets to leave anonymously around school. On the down side, if the pamphlets were gruesome, she would probably get in trouble (and by extension, I would as well!). However, distributing pamphlets has the chance to make a huge impact. Imagine if you had first discovered the horror of factory farms at, say, eight. Should I give her pamphlets to distribute (leave around school, or give to her friends)? Or should I avoid this audience?
