Archives of the old Felicifia now available online

by Pablo Stafforini on 2012-09-09T20:25:00

An archive of the old Felicifia discussion forum is now available here. Please note that some of the links are not working because the site wasn't archived in its entirety. You can also browse the site by directory structure here.

I'd like to thank Seth Baum, for creating and maintaining the original forum, as well as for preserving and sharing with us a copy of that material, and to James Evans, for making all these posts available online again. My only role was to send Seth and James some emails over the course of the last few months; they are the ones who made this happen. Thanks, guys!
