Backups and expected lifespan of Felicifia

by Brian Tomasik on 2013-01-22T11:39:00

I was just curious how long we expect Felicifia to be up and running. I hope it's for many years into the future, because otherwise people would fear that their posts would be lost. Fortunately, even if the main forum disappears, we could always specially upload the old posts like we've done for the Old Felicifia -- although this would potentially incur the cost of breaking all links ever made to this site.

BTW, the Old Felicifia is hard to browse because it doesn't have titles, but its contents can still be searched by queries of the form {suffering site:http://felicifia.org/old/www.felicifia.com/} and such.

The Internet Archive seems to have done a decent job of archiving this site (last time was May 2012), which is good. I believe there are also periodic backups of the files by Peter/James?
