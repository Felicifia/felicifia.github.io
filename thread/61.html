Felicifia mods

by Arepo on 2008-11-23T13:59:00

Prompted by FG's request, I'd like to install a few mods that would make phpBB easier to use.

Ryan, I'm particularly interested in your opinion since you're doing most of the technical work on here, but it'd be good to get requests for any additional features. You might want to check out the most popular downloads, or search this forum to see if something specific exists - but keep in mind we presumably can't use mods for phpBB 2 versions.

The list of v3 mods might be more helpful, but you can't sort them.

(bear in mind we're using phpBB 3.0.2 at the moment - Ryan, there's info on updating that might mean more to you than me on the system tab on the admin panel - though it sounds as though we'll need the password reset to be able to proceed)

Browsing the phpBB forums list, the following seem good:

Simple Syndication - RSS and ATOM Webfeed syndicator - or Simple RSS mod for phpBB3 - Will enable you to put a RSS feed on your phpBB3 Forum.

Userfriendly URL-input while posting - makes inputting URLs easier, but it's designed for phpBB2. I haven't yet found anything similar for 3, and I presume 3's not backwards compatible...

YouTube video BB code - Adds a new bbcode allowing you to easily embed videos from YouTube.com. (again, version 2 though)

Prime Self Topics - Adds a "View your topics" link next to the "View your posts" link.

Welcome PM on First Login - Allows administrators to create a message that will be sent to newly registered users upon their first login. (How do people feel about getting these? I usually find them annoying, but I suppose if they gave a useful guide to the forum they might be worth considering... (but by the time people register, will they have already looked around the forum?)

Quick reply for phpBB3 - adds quick post form at viewforum and quick reply form at viewtopic, fully customizable from ACP/UCP. Allows quick quoting of selected text and quick nick insertion at viewtopic.

Last post titles - Displays the subject of LAST POST posted in a forum (I'm very keen on this one if I understand it right - I often get interested in a forum that I'm just passing through when I see a topic title that draws me)

Genders - allows your members to specify their Gender. They can chose between "Male", "Female" and "None specified".

NB - Ryan, we should postpone selecting a default skin/designing a logo until we've selected which mods we want to use, since some (all?) of them seem skin-dependent - usually on ProSilver.

Some more...

by Arepo on 2008-11-23T14:47:00

I've just skimmed the complete list of v3 mods, and a few more caught my eye. I'm not suggesting we install all of them - maybe have a veto process, where we try to quickly rule out any that we don't like, giving a simple reason.

Advanced Meta Tags

This mod automatically generates keywords and description Meta Tags based on the topic. When no topic is being viewed, Meta Tags displays global values set in the Admin Control Panel. MOD is validated for SEO use.

Board3 Portal

Portal front page for phpBB3, complete with blocks and installer. (maybe one for later? Doesn't seem like it would be immediately beneficial... but I'm not certain I understand the description)

MOD Version Check

Adds a version check to your board to keep up to date on all your mods.

New Topic Button In Topic

Adds a 'New Topic' button into existing topics.

Prime BBCode Spoiler

This MOD will add a Spoiler BBCode, which obscures text in a box until the user clicks on it.

Prime Login via E-Mail

This MOD will let a user login with either their username or e-mail address. It will also prevent a user from registering with a username that matches another user's e-mail address.

Prime Notify PM

This MOD will insert the message body of a private message into the private message notification e-mail (and will insert the private message subject line into the subject line of the e-mail).

Prime Quick Login

This MOD will add a login area to the top of every page.

SEO MOD

Gives your forum static URL's. (can anyone tell me what the significance of this is? It doens't seem like URLs change at the moment...)

Skype Mod

Allows users to enter a skype profile which, if activated in skype, will show that users status on skype and allow other users to "call" them.

Smilie Creator

With this mod you can add some smilie signs to your forum.
Use this MOD as a custom bbcode or with a smilie creator popup.

User Blog Mod

Adds Blogs to a phpBB3 board.

View or mark unread posts

Adds a 'View unread posts' or 'You have no unread posts' link on the index page (and, for subsilver2, each other page). Also adds a 'Mark post as unread' link at the bottom of each post.

Tabulated survey at topic head

Allow users who are entitled to post polls to design surveys that will appear at the top of a topic. Users will be able to respond to the survey by clicking a button...each user response will appear at the topic head
